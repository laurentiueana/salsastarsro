Do you want to discover your salsa style and feel again the joy for dancing learning in your rhythm and in the comfort of your own home?


Now is the time to step out of your comfort zone and accept the challenge! Register for Salsa Stars Weekend, on the weekend of October 24 - 25, and participate in 10 online workshops held by 5 Top Salseras in Romania: Alexandra Ilie, Judit Borzasi, Carolina Capitan, Monica Dobroiu and Alexandra Ichim.


As everyone knows Brasov is well known for its united salsa community, Stars Salsa Weekend is launched at the initiative of the two representatives of the famous Salsa schools in Brasov: Salsa Picante and Step in2 Salsa: Moni and Ali. Being salsa instructors with more than 10 years of experience, Alexandra Ichim and Monica Dobroiu created this event out of a desire to share the knowledge, technique and love for salsa of the best salsa dancers in Romania


The First Edition Stars Salsa Weekend wants to guide you to discover the style that characterizes you best, offering value through the quality of the instructors who come in front of you, offering 10 online courses, ALL LEVELS.

Benefits:

  • Discover your style;

  • Gain self-esteem on the dance floor;
  • Take your time to learn all the moves;
  • Learn in your own comfortable environment;
  • Train your mind to learn and remember all the new elements;
  • Learn from THE BEST, dedicated and experienced from ROMANIA;

What you gain:

  • Go through new elements that completes the basics of Salsa On1, Salsa On2, Mambo, Afro, Boogaloo/Cha Cha Cha;

All these ingredients that bring flavor in dance, are elements that will guide you to shine both on the dance floor and on stage.
